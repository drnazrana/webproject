Dr. Nazrana Ghayyur

Dr Nazrana Ghayyur

Dr. Nazrana Ghayyur MBBS MD FMAS is specialist Obstetrician and Gynaecologist currently serving as Director of Obstetrics and Gynaecology unit at H.M. Surgical Hospital Azamgarh.

She has done her MD in Obstetrics & Gynaecology from world renowned institution Queen Mary Hospital and George’s Medical University Lucknow India. During her residency keen interests in treatment of acute issues such as Obstetrics Emergency has lead her to bag coveted C S Dawn Gold Medal in 2022. Fellowship In Minimal Access Surgery (FMAS).

Dr. Nazrana Ghayyur is an Assistant Professor (Adhoc) at Medical College (PGI) Azamgarh since 2024, previously serving as the Registrar from 2022 to 2024, where she contributed to achieving the largest number of Natural Birth and Operative deliveries for a single year.

She specializes in dealing with medical disorders in pregnancy and high-risk cases, demonstrating high efficiency in managing Normal and High-risk pregnancies, early pregnancy complications, bleeding from or into genital tracts, restricted growth of the fetus, multiple gestation, and recurrent pregnancy loss. Dr. Nazrana Ghayyur is also an expert in managing medical disorders in pregnancy such as Diabetes, Thyroid, Liver disorders, Kidney problems, Heart disease, TB, and STI.
